EclipseCon 2006 Was A Great Success

EclipseCon is the premier technical and user conference focusing on the power of the Eclipse platform. EclipseCon 2006 was a great success - more developers, more users, more technical sessions, more open source projects, more exhibitors, more parties, and just plain more of everything. We hope that you were one of the sell-out crowd in attendance, but if you were not, plan to join us at a future conference!

The presentations for most (but not quite all) of the sessions are available online here. We hope you find them informative and useful.
