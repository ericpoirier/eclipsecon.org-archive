EclipseCon 2009: Smaller But Better!

EclipseCon 2009 was a little smaller (thanks to company travel restrictions), but those of us who made it to Santa Clara experienced the best EclipseCon yet. The hallways were crowded, the sessions were fantastic, and the evenings at the remodeled bar were the place to be.
