EclipseCon 2008 Was A Great Succcess

EclipseCon 2008 was a great success - more technical sessions, more open source projects, more BOFs, more parties, and just plain more. Plus we had the exciting IBM and Blackberry Developer Days, the OSGi DevCon and an OMG Symposium. We hope that you were there, but if you were not, plan join us at the next EclipseCon!
